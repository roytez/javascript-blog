Room

Nice apartment just a few steps from the beach with ancient places nearby- Corinth, Acrocorinth etc. The apartment includes: kitchen connected with the living room with one double sofa, bathroom, one room with 2 single beds and a balcony with the view to the sea.

The closest beach (&taverna) is about 50m from the apartment, whereas the main beach with sunbeds to rent, luna park, big variety of restaurants and bars are just 300m from the place. The place can fit max. 4 adult people + 1 child.
